Experience

I am a bassist from Reading, PA. A graduate of Penn State University with degrees in English, Music Performance, and Music Studies, I have extensive experience performing various genres of music.

My playing experience includes, but is not limited to:
Berks Sinfonietta (Principal), The Reading Pops Orchestra (Principal), Genesius Theater, Lehigh Valley Chamber Orchestra, Allegro Orchestra, Pennsylvania Philharmonic, Penn State Chamber Orchestra, Penn State Philharmonic, Penn State School of Theater, Berks Opera Workshop, Reading Choral Society, Central Pennsylvania Symphony, bass instruction at Berks Summer Strings Institute and Reading Symphony Junior Strings Orchestra.

Musical theater shows that I have performed included, but are not limited to: Kinky Boots, Raisin the Musical, Les Mis, School of Rock, The Little Mermaid, The Rocky Horror Picture Show, and the World Premiere of Broadway writer, Bill Russell's, Has Anyone Seen My Mind.

Education

I studied under Robert Nairn during my studies at Penn State University and earned degrees in Double Bass Performance as well as Music Studies.

As a student, I had the opportunity to meet and learn from many bassists including, but not limited to, Kurt Muroki, Chi-chi Nwanoku, Barry Green, Andreas Bennetzen, Robert Oppelt, and Tim Dunin.

About Me

A native of Reading, Pennsylvania, I regularly perform throughout eastern Pennsylvania. I play double bass as well as bass guitar in a variety of settings.

I am also the fortunate winner of the 2025 International Society of Bassists Build-a-Bass sweepstakes. As such, I am the proud owner of a one-of-a-kind, world class instrument that has been built by a team of renowned luthiers, under the leadership of Paul Hart.
